FIRST MONTHSARY

This was our first monthsary, and also the 2nd time I gave you a bouquet of flowers. I still remember how happy and radiant you were—it made me feel so fulfilled. I just want to remind you how much I truly love you, every single day. That day was simple but unforgettable. Before we went out, you even cooked macaroni spaghetti for us.
